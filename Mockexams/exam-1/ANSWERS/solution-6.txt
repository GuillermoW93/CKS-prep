6. Kube-bench & Fixing CIS Benchmarks

If kube-bench fails because the Kubelet allows anonymous authentication, you must disable it.

Edit /var/lib/kubelet/config.yaml (On the Node):
Change enabled to false under authentication.anonymous:

YAML
authentication:
  anonymous:
    enabled: false
Restart the Kubelet:

Bash
systemctl daemon-reload
systemctl restart kubelet
